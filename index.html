OWN OR MANAGE RENTAL PROPERTY?

 Investment/Rental property poses a challenge for the owner and the craftsman. You as the owner/property manager, have the need to maintain the property and its appeal to the potential renter; however you probably do not want to spend a large amount of money. Rental property often times gets additional abuse that would not normally occur in your own home. The manager’s objective is to maintain reasonable quality appearance, property value and function, while keeping expenses within strict budget limits to realize a profitable year end income.

“MUCCI & SON CONTRACTORS”can help you achieve your goal.

  • We can often repair broken cabinets, repair bedroom doors, install new locks and maintain the high traffic areas that get the most abuse.

  • Wall repairs and sanding in the common areas increase appeal, and psychologically make the renter more aware of “being careful” when traveling through the halls and stairwells. Nice surfaces will stay nicer longer, which will maintain your investment.

  • Walls damaged from abuse, or water can be efficiently repaired with our dustless sanding system.

  • When a renter moves out, we can repair, sand and paint walls, clean carpet and general cleaning in several days. Erik will manage all aspects of the process. Leftover junk can be removed and in some cases hauled away from you property.

  • We can maintain what you have or complete a full “REMODEL.”
