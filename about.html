ERIK MUCCI

FOUNDER OF MUCCI & SON

Erik is a graduate of Purdue University, with a BS degree in “Construction Management” and OSHA certification (30 hour). While attending school, residential building construction is where he focused his time and energy. New construction of multi–unit complexes and “Build to Spec Luxury Homes” was his specialty.

After graduation, Erik’s professional construction projects included

  • extensive residential home remodeling
  • custom single family homes construction
  • the construction of multi-family condominium complexes
New construction projects required his talents to obtain all building permits and assure compliance, oversee all represented construction trades, negotiate contracts and complete walk through with the customer.
